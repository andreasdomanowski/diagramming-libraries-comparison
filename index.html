Comparing Diagramming Libraries for the Generation of Editors Based on Meta Models

A part of my diploma thesis (Model-Driven, (Semi-)Automatic Generation of Lightweight, Client-Side, Online Diagram Editors) deals with the generation of client-side diagram editors based on meta models. For finding a suitable and flexible library for this task, two of the most promising candidates (diagram-js and JointJS) are evaluated.

This landing page contains links to the automatically built and deployed evaluation projects. For more information, check out the repository.
